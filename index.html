Optimizing an AI to Identify Particle Collisions

Without Starting From Scratch

Transfer learning strategies for jet tagging at the LHC with pretrained foundation models. Part of the UC San Diego MAP (Mentor Assistance Program) undergraduate research program at the San Diego Supercomputer Center.

A joint research project by Raunav Mendiratta & Emmet Muschenetz, with mentorship from Ellison Scheuller and Jason Weitz, under the direction of Dr. Javier Duarte.
