# Optimizing an AI to Identify Particle Collisions

**Without Starting From Scratch**

A joint research project by **Raunav Mendiratta** and **Emmet Muschenetz**, with mentorship from Ellison Scheuller and Jason Weitz, under the direction of [Dr. Javier Duarte](https://jduarte.physics.ucsd.edu/). Part of the UC San Diego [MAP](https://education.sdsc.edu/studenttech/map-home/) (Mentor Assistance Program) undergraduate research program at the San Diego Supercomputer Center.

## About

This project investigates transfer learning strategies for jet tagging at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) using Sophon, a pretrained foundation model. Rather than training a classifier from scratch, we adapt Sophon's pretrained representations to a 10-class jet tagging task — achieving 99.1% of state-of-the-art accuracy at 1/60th the trainable parameter count.
